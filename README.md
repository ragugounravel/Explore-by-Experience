# Experience-Led Discovery for UNRAVEL

**Author:** Raguram Narayanaswamy  
**Role Context:** Product Strategy Proposal  

---

## 📌 Overview

UNRAVEL’s current homepage is optimized for transactional, price-sensitive, destination-driven search.  

The existing flow assumes:

> "User knows WHERE they want to go."

However, emerging premium segments increasingly think:

> "I know WHY I want to travel. Help me discover WHERE."

This proposal introduces an **experience-led discovery layer** to reposition UNRAVEL from a booking utility to an inspiration-driven travel platform.

---

## 1️⃣ The Problem — The Discovery Gap

### Current State
- Transactional interface
- Price-led comparison
- Destination-first navigation

### User Shift
High-value segments are increasingly intent-driven rather than geography-driven.

### Impacted Segments
- **Premium cardholders** seeking exclusivity and aesthetic alignment  
- **Gen Z travelers** prioritizing social-first, meaningful travel  
- **40+ travelers** seeking spiritual or cultural immersion  

### Core Issue
UNRAVEL competes primarily on price instead of differentiation.

---

## 2️⃣ Strategic Insight

Travel behavior is shifting:

Destination-first → Intent-first
Utility platform → Lifestyle curator


Users are asking:

> "What kind of experience do I want?"

Not:

> "Where should I go?"

### Opportunity

Reposition UNRAVEL as an **inspiration engine**, not just a booking utility.

---

## 3️⃣ The Solution — Explore by Experience

### Proposed Homepage Module
- **Placement:** Between hero banner and standard property cards  
- **Name:** Explore by Experience  

### Powered By
- Existing metadata tags  
- Dynamic property curation  

### Initial Categories
- Architectural Marvels  
- Spiritual Retreats  
- Cultural Heritage  
- Historical & “Once in a Lifetime”  

### UX Shift

Instead of asking:
> "Where do you want to go?"

We ask:
> "What kind of journey are you seeking?"

### Prototype

- **Explore by Experience** - [Demo link](https://ragugounravel.github.io/Explore-by-Experience/)

---

## 4️⃣ Why This Works

### ✅ Low Technical Risk
- Uses existing property tags  
- No SEO disruption  
- No structural search flow changes  

### ✅ Behavioral Alignment
Reduces cognitive load for:
- Browsers  
- Indecisive travelers  
- Premium inspiration-seeking users  

### ✅ Emotional Repositioning

Cheap booking engine → Experience curator

---

## 5️⃣ Hypothesis

If users discover by intent rather than geography:

- 📈 Homepage CTR increases by 12–18%
- 📈 Premium inventory views increase by 15%
- 📈 AOV increases by 8–12%
- 📈 Session depth increases by 10%+

---

## 6️⃣ MVP & Experiment Plan

### Phase 1: A/B Test (20% Traffic)

**Variant A:** Existing homepage  
**Variant B:** Homepage + Experience module  

### Metrics to Track
- CTR per module  
- Save rate  
- Add-to-cart rate  
- AOV  
- Bounce rate  
- Premium property share  

---

### Phase 2: Behavioral Personalization

If user clicks "Spiritual Retreats":

- Retarget with similar properties  
- Show spiritual collections in future sessions
